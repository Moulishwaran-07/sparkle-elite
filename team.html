OUR TEAM

Smart, witty, detailed-oriented, and mission-focused, our team meets the mark every time.
We are honest and transparent with our clients. We respect the beliefs of
our diverse clientele and we care deeply about the work we do.

Tom Berry

(founder)

I started sparkle elite in 2024 because I knew that nonprofits needed help through my own board and volunteer work. I am an entrepreneur by nature and starting my own businesses was a natural progression of my goals and skills. I really listen to my clients to understand their needs. So many nonprofits need assistance with creating and executing a successful fundraising event. You can have a committed set of volunteers but you also need expertise, organization and knowledge of fundraising principles to really knock it out of the park.

John Wick

(director of special events)

john is committed to bringing the fun in fundraising for all sparkle elite clients. Him passion for event planning is intertwined with him love of being able to be a part of so many amazing charitable organizations. john goal is to collaborate with organizations and bring them peace of mind during the planning process. Being able to put on a successful event while bringing a community closer together is her goal. he is excited to be spearheading the Southern California initiative of bringing sparkle elite nonprofit event planning expertise to organizations in india.

Parker Lee

(producer of special events)

parker developed him passion for fundraising and event planning while serving on a local nonprofit board over the last four years. he has managed the planning and execution of a variety of fundraising programs including auction events, corporate sponsorships, and fun runs. What he enjoys most about working with nonprofits is building relationships and community involvement.

Roman Axe

(senior of special events)

roman enjoys working with nonprofits because they help the community grow in positive ways. With him giving spirit and grateful attitude, roman ties him love for serving the community with him knack for event planning. he loves channeling him creative skills, work ethic, and organizational skills to help nonprofits reach their goals. he especially loves meeting with clients, hearing their vision, and bringing that vision to fruition. To roman, the most rewarding part of planning non-profit events is that moment on event day where through the fast-paced energy on the day of, he gets to take a moment and cherish people from all walks of life uniting under one roof towards a common goal.
